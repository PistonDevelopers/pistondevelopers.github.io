Functions

blur

Perfomrs a Gausian blur on pixels width and height are the dimensions of the buffer. sigma is a meausure of how much to blur by.
box_kernel

Calculate the box kernel. When applied in two dimensions with a support of 0.5 it is equivalent to nearest neighbor sampling.
catmullrom_kernel

Calculate the Catmull-Rom cubic spline. Also known as a form of BiCubic sampling in two dimensions.
filter3x3

Perform a 3x3 box filter on pixels. width is the width of pixels. height is the height of pixels. kernel is an array of the filter weights of length 9.
gaussian

The Gaussian Function. r is the standard deviation.
gaussian_kernel

Calculate the gaussian function with a standard deviation of 1.0
lanczos3_kernel

Calculate the lanczos kernel with a window of 3
resize

Resize pixels. width and height are the original dimensions. nwidth and nheight are the new dimensions. filter is the sampling filter to use.
triangle_kernel

Calculate the triangle function. Also known as BiLinear sampling in two dimensions.
